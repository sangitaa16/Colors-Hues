15 Quotes From The Office That Will Stick With Us Forever

Loved by many and critically acclaimed for most of its run, The Office (U.S.) is a remarkable sitcom that repeatedly broke the fourth wall and introduced the world to a group of memorable characters. Throughout its 9 season run, 'The Office' had countless memorable and funny quotes. Being a huge fan of the show myself, I wanted to create a website where people could mouse over the characters from 'The Office' and get their infamous quotes. The quote will not only appear down below, but its audio will also play. To make this a bit more fun, I also added funny gifs - They will appear when you mouse over the images as well.
